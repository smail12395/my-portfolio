I’m an aspiring Full Stack Developer with a strong passion for creating clean, responsive, and accessible web applications. I enjoy bringing user interfaces to life with thoughtful design and solid front-end development, while continuously building my skills on the back-end to become a well-rounded developer.
My main focus right now is mastering modern tools and frameworks like JavaScript, React,Tailwind CSS, and exploring back-end technologies like Tailwind CSS , Express, and MongoDB . I’m especially interested in understanding how performance, usability, and accessibility come together to shape great user experiences.
As a creative problem solver, I approach every project as an opportunity to learn and grow. Whether I’m designing pixel-perfect layouts or connecting front-end to back-end logic, I strive to build projects that are both functional and user-friendly.
Outside of coding, I love exploring new design trends, reading about software architecture, and studying how great developers build scalable systems. I'm excited to continue learning, collaborating, and building real-world applications that make a difference.
